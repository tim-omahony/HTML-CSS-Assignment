our work


All our services are free of charge to those that need them. Our friendly volunteers ensure that our service users are well looked after on a daily basis.
Our doors are always open. There has been a huge increase in homelessness and mental health issues that are affecting so many people which have forced them to avail of our services.
Our volunteers come from all walks of life; nurses, truck drivers,scientists, general operatives, musicians, teachers, students, to name but a few, and Cork Simon Community has the opportunity to provide these services thanks to the kind help of our volunteers and donators.
